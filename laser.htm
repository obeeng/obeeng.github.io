Oberon Engineering’s Laser Division is a cut above the rest (pardon the pun!), with our investment in the latest laser and waterjet cutting technology.

Our precision laser cutting includes Mild Steel, Stainless Steel, Bissalloy, Plywood, MDF Board, Masonite, Lexcen (Marguard) and Perspex, in just about any thickness, from 0.125mm-20mm.

And when it comes to waterjet cutting, what we can cut (up to 120mm) is pretty much limited only by your imagination.

It’s probably easier to ask what we can’t cut (Answer: Very little!).

So if you need something complex or simple precision cut, from stone to steel, glass to granite, copper, brass, tiles, white iron, aluminium (and just about anything else you can think of), Oberon Engineering has the edge.

 

To discuss your requirements, contact us for expert advice, outstanding personal and professional service and a top quality solution to your requirements.
